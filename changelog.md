## 🚀 v1.0.0 - Initial Release (2026-03-28)

## ✨ Features & Additions:
* **Added the Sturdy Hammer:** A heavy-duty, custom 3D-modeled tool designed for manual forging.
  
* **Manual Forging Mechanic:** Sneak + Right-Click on a Depot holding an ingot to instantly smash it into a sheet. 
  
* **Dynamic Recipe Support:** The hammer automatically detects and supports *all* valid Mechanical Press recipes. It works immediately with Iron, Gold, Copper, Brass, and even ingots from other mods.
